## Display

- Tailwind uses the traditional flexbox conventions 
    - (unlike bootstrap, which uses its own `container>row>column` system)
- 

```html
<!-- samples -->
<div>
    <!-- block  -->
  <div class="block">

    <div class="flex"> flex 1
    </div>
    <div class="flex"> flex 2
    </div>

  </div>
</div>
```
